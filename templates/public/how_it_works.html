The Process

A breakdown of how ePery works

•  Sellers
1.) Put their house on ePery which allows buyers to bid on it
2.) Bids ARE NOT contractually obligated, they are a means of introducing buyers and sellers
3.) Seller's can then see who is bidding and have access to that bidders info such as

- photos
- current address
- profession
- how many houses they have bid on
- reason for looking for a house in your neighborhood
- how they will buy your house (cash or loan)
- if they are pre-approved for a loan and how much
- what their interests are, if they have a family, etc.
- ability to communicate with them through messaging system

- Coming soon: schedule open houses and times you will privately show the house
- Coming soon: ability to upload property inspection documents
- Coming soon: message board for negotiations (who will repair what)

•  Buyers
1.) Can bid on any house
2.) Given many data points to know if price of house is good
3.) Interact with the seller through messaging system
4.) Can negotiate
5.) Request private showings with seller
