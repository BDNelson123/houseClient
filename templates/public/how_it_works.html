How it Works

A simple solution to an age old problem

Purpose of ePerty

Our sole purpose is to connect sellers of houses with interested buyers.

•  The real question you should be asking is "What do real estate agents do?"
- determine the best price to sell the house
- market the house to interested buyers
- introduce interested buyers to the seller & brokering deals between them
- process all paperwork for the sale of your house

•  The next question should be ...
"Why can't I do this myself online and not pay 6% of the sale to a real estate agent?"

Finding the Right Price

Pricing your house correctly could save you months of heartache.

•  Sellers
- ePerty uses data from multiple sources to help you price your house correctly
- You are given our estimate, the current tax valuation, related house prices, etc.
•  Buyers
- Will see these valuations and know if your asking price is high or low
- Will be given analytics about the neighborhood and its property valuations
